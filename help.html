Getting Started

Welcome to the Local Community Event Portal! This guide will help you navigate and use all the features of our portal.

Event Registration

To register for an event:

  1. Fill out the registration form with your details
  2. Select your preferred event from the dropdown menu
  3. Choose a date that works for you
  4. Add any special requirements in the message box
  5. Click the Register button to submit

Finding Nearby Events

The "Find Nearby Events" feature uses your device's location to show you events in your area. When you click the button:

  • Allow location access when prompted
  • Your coordinates will be used to find the closest events
  • Results will be displayed on the page

Image Gallery

Our event gallery shows images from past events:

  • Double-click any image to enlarge it
  • Click again to return to normal size
  • Hover over images to see event titles

User Preferences

Your preferences are automatically saved:

  • Your last selected event type is remembered
  • Use the "Clear Preferences" button to reset all saved data
  • Preferences are stored locally on your device
